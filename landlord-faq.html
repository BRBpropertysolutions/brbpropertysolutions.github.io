Landlord Frequently Asked Questions

Who pays the rent?
BRB Property Solutions leases the property directly and pays rent under the terms of the agreement, regardless of occupancy.
Who manages the tenants?
We take full responsibility for management, inspections, and liaison with local authorities.
Will my property be used as social housing?
Properties are supplied to local authorities for temporary or emergency accommodation in line with agreed standards and safeguarding requirements.
What about wear and tear?
Properties are inspected regularly, and condition standards are clearly set out within the lease agreement.
Is this rent-to-rent?
This is a professional leasing arrangement with a compliant operator, not a casual rent-to-rent model.
Can I exit the agreement?
Exit provisions are clearly defined within the lease to provide clarity for both parties.
