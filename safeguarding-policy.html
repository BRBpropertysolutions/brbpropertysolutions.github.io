Safeguarding Policy

BRB Property Solutions is committed to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of individuals occupying accommodation we manage, including vulnerable adults and families placed through local authority arrangements.

Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and representatives involved in property management, inspections, and operational delivery.

Our Position

BRB Property Solutions does not provide regulated care or support services. However, we recognise that individuals placed in temporary or emergency accommodation may be vulnerable and require safeguarding awareness.

Safeguarding Awareness

Staff are expected to remain alert to indicators of abuse, neglect, exploitation, or significant risk to health or safety encountered during routine duties.

Reporting Concerns

Any safeguarding concern identified will be reported promptly to the relevant local authority placement team, safeguarding department, or emergency services where appropriate.

Working With Local Authorities

We cooperate fully with local authority safeguarding frameworks, inspections, and investigations and provide information where reasonably required.

Review

This policy is reviewed regularly to ensure alignment with best practice and local authority expectations.
