Safeguarding Policy

BRB Property Solutions is committed to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of all individuals occupying accommodation we manage, including vulnerable adults and families placed through local authority arrangements.

Our Commitment

While BRB Property Solutions does not provide regulated care or support services, we recognise that residents placed in temporary or emergency accommodation may be vulnerable. We therefore operate with safeguarding awareness and take reasonable steps to identify and report safeguarding concerns.

Scope

This policy applies to all staff, contractors, and representatives involved in property management, inspections, and day-to-day operational activities.

Safeguarding Awareness

Staff are expected to remain alert to potential safeguarding indicators, including but not limited to signs of abuse, neglect, exploitation, or significant risk to health or safety.

Reporting Concerns

Any safeguarding concerns identified during the course of our activities will be reported promptly to the relevant local authority department, placement team, or emergency services where appropriate.

Working With Local Authorities

BRB Property Solutions cooperates fully with local authority safeguarding frameworks, inspections, and investigations and will provide relevant information where required.

Review

This policy is reviewed regularly to ensure ongoing alignment with best practice and local authority expectations.
