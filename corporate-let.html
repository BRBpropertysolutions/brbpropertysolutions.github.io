What Is a Corporate Let?

A corporate let is a long-term arrangement where BRB Property Solutions Ltd leases your property directly and acts as your tenant.

We then supply the property to local authorities as part of their housing provision, while managing all day-to-day responsibilities on your behalf.

How It Works

  • Property suitability assessment
  • Long-term agreement (typically 2+ years)
  • Guaranteed rent paid monthly
  • Full management, maintenance, and compliance
  • Property returned in agreed condition at end of term

Key Benefits for Landlords

  • Guaranteed monthly rent
  • No void periods
  • No tenant management
  • No council interaction required
  • Long-term income stability
