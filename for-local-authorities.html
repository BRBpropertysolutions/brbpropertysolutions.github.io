Housing Solutions for Local Authorities

BRB Property Solutions works in partnership with local authorities to deliver professionally managed temporary, emergency, and social housing in line with statutory requirements, safeguarding expectations, and governance standards.

What We Provide

Temporary & Emergency Accommodation

Professionally managed accommodation supplied to meet short-term and emergency housing demand, maintained in line with Housing Act standards and council inspection frameworks.

Corporate Leasing & Guaranteed Rent

Long-term leasing arrangements providing stability, predictable costs, and reduced management burden for local authority partners.

Property Management & Compliance

End-to-end property management including inspections, maintenance coordination, compliance monitoring, and responsive issue resolution.

Governance, Compliance & Assurance

Property Redress Scheme (PRS)

We are registered members of the Property Redress Scheme, providing independent complaints resolution in line with regulatory requirements.

Safeguarding Awareness

We recognise the vulnerabilities often associated with temporary and emergency accommodation and operate with safeguarding awareness, reporting pathways, and cooperation with council safeguarding teams.

Transparent & Ethical Delivery

Our services are delivered with clear governance, accountability, and ethical standards to support council oversight and resident wellbeing.
